## DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF EMBODIMENTS OF THE INVENTION

- describe process for producing and purifying 68Gallium
- irradiate target solution with accelerated particle beam
- dilute irradiated target solution in water
- feed diluted solution into strong cation exchanger
- wash strong cation exchanger to remove impurities
- elute Zinc isotopes from strong cation exchanger
- wash strong cation exchanger again to remove acetone and HBr
- elute 68Gallium from strong cation exchanger
- feed eluted solution into strong anion exchanger
- wash strong anion exchanger to remove impurities
- elute highly purified and concentrated 68Gallium isotope
